# Exercise Coach SMS Program

Uses SMS to send participant-facing program messages, reminders, and support updates for an educational/research coaching program.

Participants may receive onboarding prompts, periodic check-ins, and follow-up messages related to their participation.

## Opt-In

Users join only after providing explicit consent through our enrollment process.

Opt-in is collected before any recurring SMS communication begins.

## Opt-Out and Help

- Reply STOP to +1 (855) 906-1132 to opt out at any time.
- Reply HELP to +1 (855) 906-1132 for support information.

After STOP, users receive a confirmation and no further non-essential messages.

## Message Frequency

Message frequency varies by program activity and user participation.

Typical volume is approximately 1-5 messages per day during active periods.

## Rates

Message and data rates may apply.

## Privacy

Participant data is used only to operate and support this SMS program in accordance with applicable research and privacy requirements.

Program data is limited to what is needed for messaging operations, participant support, and approved program analysis.
